Projects

VLMPlanner: VLM-based Motion Planning for Autonomous Driving

  • Used vision-language models to reason about fine-grained traffic conditions from multi-view onboard images.
  • Designed a scene-complexity-aware inference mechanism to improve safety and robustness while reducing real-time computational overhead.
  • Validated the method with open-loop and closed-loop experiments in nuPlan.

Unified 3D Latent and Action Diffusion Model

  • Jointly diffused 3D foundation model features and robot actions for geometry-aware action prediction.
  • Evaluated the approach in LIBERO and RoboTwin, and on UR5e and Kinova Gen3 real-robot platforms.

Training-free and Plug-and-play Pruning for VLA Models

  • Designed a feature-similarity-based update policy to decide when sparse patterns should be refreshed across adjacent visual observations.
  • Introduced temporal-consistency feature aggregation to identify redundant channels more stably.
  • Scheduled pruning computation into VLA inference idle intervals and implemented sparse Linear layers with Triton.
  • Evaluated on OpenVLA-OFT, π0.5, and CogACT across simulation and real-robot platforms.

Dual-arm UR5e Robot Teleoperation and Model Deployment

  • Developed a teleoperation system for dual-arm UR5e robots using Meta Quest 3.
  • Deployed ACT, π0, and related policy models.

Chemistry Embodied Manipulation Dataset and Benchmark

  • Developed a teleoperation and data-recording system using Manus gloves and VIVE Trackers for Franka arm and XHand control.
  • Designed atomic operations such as grasping, placing, pressing, shaking, and stirring for medium- and long-horizon tasks.
  • Migrated real-world scenes into Isaac Sim for scalable data collection.
